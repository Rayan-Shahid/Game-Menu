Instructions:

Welcome to the "Red Light, Green Light" Challenge! Your goal is to successfully reach the other end of the enclosed field within the Three-minute time limit. But be cautious, the robotic doll is watching!

CONTROLS:

To move forward, press the W key.

To move backward, press the S key.

To move left, press the A key.

To move right, press the D key.
